Frequently Asked Questions

What algorithms are supported?
We support all standard JWT algorithms: HMAC (HS256, HS384, HS512), RSA (RS256, RS384, RS512), and ECDSA (ES256, ES384, ES512). The demo shows HS256, but Pro unlocks all.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. All JWT generation happens in your browser or via secure API. Secrets and private keys are encrypted at rest and never logged or shared.
Can I verify existing tokens?
Yes! Paste any JWT token to decode and verify its signature. You'll see the header, payload, and validation status with detailed error messages if invalid.
